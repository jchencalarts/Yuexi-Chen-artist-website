My name is (Jessica)Yuexi Chen, I am currently a BFA 2 in the Fine Arts Program at California Institute of the Arts. I was born in Chengdu, China and lived there for eleven years. I moved to New York in 2015, where I stayed until moving to California in 2022 for my BFA Education at Calarts. My Metier at Calarts is Fine Arts; my main practice includes multimedia art as well as traditional art. I create abstract pieces that are focused on fluidity and form.

My past works utilized more traditional mediums such as oil paint, acrylic paint, ink and watercolor. My more recent works have been multimedia. For example, some pieces are time-based, structure oriented and installations.
